\(A^c \cap B^c\)

\(\left( {A \cup B} \right) \cap C\)

(f)

\(\left( {A \cap C} \right) \cup \left( {B \cap C} \right)\)

(g)

\(B \cap D\)

(h)

\(\left( {B \cap D} \right)^c\)

(i)

\(A - D\)

(j)

\(B - D\)

(k)

\(\left( A - D \right) \cup \left( B - D \right)\)

(l)

\(\left( A \cup B \right) - D\)
