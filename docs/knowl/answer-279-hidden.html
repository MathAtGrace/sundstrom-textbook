Let \(x \in B\text{.}\) For each \(\alpha \in \Lambda\text{,}\) \(B \subseteq A_\alpha\) and, hence, \(x \in A_\alpha\text{.}\) This means that for each \(\alpha \in \Lambda\text{,}\) \(x \in A_\alpha\) and, hence, \(x \in \bigcap\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\text{.}\) Therefore, \(B \subseteq \bigcap\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\text{.}\)
