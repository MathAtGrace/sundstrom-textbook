Item 3.

It is important to remember that the inductive step in an induction proof is a proof of a conditional statement. Although we did not explicitly use the forward-backward process in the inductive step for Proposition 4.1.5, it was implicitly used in the discussion prior to Proposition 4.1.5. The key question was, “How does knowing the sum of the first \(k\) squares help us find the sum of the first \(\left( {k + 1} \right)\) squares?”
