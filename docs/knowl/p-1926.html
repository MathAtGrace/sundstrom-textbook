Exercise 11 in Section 3.1 gave three important properties of congruence modulo \(n\text{.}\) Because of their importance, these properties are stated and proved in Theorem 3.5.7. Please remember that textbook proofs are usually written in final form of “reporting the news.” Before reading these proofs, it might be instructive to first try to construct a know-show table for each proof.
