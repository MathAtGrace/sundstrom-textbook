Since \(\left( {5m + 1} \right)\) is an integer, equation (4.10) shows that 4 divides \(\left( {5^{k + 1} - 1} \right)\text{.}\) Therefore, if \(P( k )\) is true, then \(P( k + 1 )\) is true and the inductive step has been established. Thus, by Principle 4.3, for every natural number \(n\text{,}\) 4 divides \(\left( {5^n - 1} \right)\text{.}\) \(\blacksquare\)
