Define the relation \(\sim\) on \(\Q\) as follows: For \(a, b \in \Q\text{,}\) \(a \sim b\) if and only if \(a - b \in \Z\text{.}\) In Progress Check 7.13 of Section 7.2, we showed that the relation \(\sim\) is an equivalence relation on \(\Q\text{.}\) Also, see Exercise 9 in Section 7.2.
