Exercise 7.

Let \(\Lambda\) be a nonempty indexing set and let \(\mathscr{A} = \left\{ A_\alpha \mid \alpha \in \Lambda \right\}\) be an indexed family of sets. Also, assume that \(\Gamma \subseteq \Lambda\) and \(\Gamma \ne \emptyset\text{.}\) Prove that

(a)

\(\bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Gamma}^{}A_\alpha \subseteq \bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\)

(b)

\(\bigcap\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha \subseteq \bigcap\limits_{\alpha \in \Gamma}^{}A_\alpha\)
