For statements \(P\) and \(Q\text{,}\) use truth tables to determine if each of the following statements is a tautology, a contradiction, or neither.

(a)

\(\mynot Q \vee (P \to Q)\text{.}\)

(b)

\(Q \wedge (P \wedge \mynot Q)\text{.}\)

(c)

\((Q \wedge P) \wedge (P \to \mynot Q)\text{.}\)

(d)

\(\mynot Q \to (P \wedge \mynot P)\text{.}\)
