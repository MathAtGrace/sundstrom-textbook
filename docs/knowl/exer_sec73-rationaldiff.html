Exercise 7.

Define the relation \(\sim\) on \(\R\) as follows:

For \(x, y \in \R\text{,}\) \(x \sim y\) if and only if \(x - y \in \Q\text{.}\)

(a)

Prove that \(\sim\) is an equivalence relation on \(\R\text{.}\)

(b)

List four different real numbers that are in the equivalence class of \(\sqrt{2}\text{.}\)

(c)

If \(a \in \Q\text{,}\) what is the equivalence class of \(a\text{?}\)

(d)

Prove that \(\left[ \sqrt{2} \right] = \left\{ r + \sqrt{2} \mid r \in \Q \right\}\text{.}\)

(e)

If \(a \in \Q\text{,}\) prove that there is a bijection from \(\left[ a \right]\) to \(\left[ \sqrt{2} \right]\text{.}\)
