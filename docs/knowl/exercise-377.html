Exercise 5.

Is the following conjecture true or false?

Conjecture 4.28.

Let \(f_1, f_2, \ldots, f_m, \ldots\) be the sequence of the Fibonacci numbers. For each natural number \(n\text{,}\) the numbers \(f_nf_{n+3}\text{,}\) \(2f_{n+1}f_{n+2}\text{,}\) and \(\left( f_{n+1}^2 + f_{n+2}^2 \right)\) form a Pythagorean triple.
