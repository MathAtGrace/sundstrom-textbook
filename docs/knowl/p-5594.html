Let \(A\text{,}\) \(B\text{,}\) and \(C\) be sets.

  1. For each set \(A\text{,}\) \(A \approx A\text{.}\)

  2. For all sets \(A\) and \(B\text{,}\) if \(A \approx B\text{,}\) then \(B \approx A\text{.}\)

  3. For all sets \(A\text{,}\) \(B\text{,}\) and \(C\text{,}\) if \(A \approx B\) and \(B \approx C\text{,}\) then \(A \approx C\text{.}\)
