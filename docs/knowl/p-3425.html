For each natural number \(n\text{,}\) let \(A_n = \left\{ 1, n, n^2 \right\}\text{.}\) For example,

\begin{equation*} A_1 = \left\{ 1 \right\} \qquad A_2 = \left\{ 1, 2, 4 \right\} \qquad A_3 = \left\{ 1, 3, 9 \right\}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \bigcup\limits_{j=1}^{3}A_j = \left\{1, 2, 3, 4, 9 \right\} \qquad \bigcap\limits_{j=1}^{3}A_j = \left\{1 \right\} \end{equation*}
