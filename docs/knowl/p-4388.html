Let \(a\) and \(b\) be integers with \(a \ne 0\) and \(b > 0\text{.}\) Then \(\gcd( {a, b} )\) is the only natural number \(d\) such that

  1. \(d\) divides \(a\) and \(d\) divides \(b\text{,}\) and

  2. if \(k\) is an integer that divides both \(a\) and \(b\text{,}\) then \(k\) divides \(d\text{.}\)
